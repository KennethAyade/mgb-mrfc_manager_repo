## Introduction

### What is the MGB MRFC Manager App?

The MGB MRFC Manager app helps manage:
- **MRFCs** (Multi-Partite Monitoring Teams)
- **Meetings** and their records
- **Documents** and compliance reports
- **User access** and permissions

### Who Can Use This App?

There are three types of users:

1. **Regular Users** - Can view information and make proposals
2. **Admins** - Can manage meetings and documents
3. **Super Admins** - Can do everything, including creating users

### System Requirements

- Android tablet (10-inch recommended)
- Android version 7.0 or higher
- Internet connection
- Camera (for attendance photos)
- Microphone (for voice recordings, admins only)

## Tips and Best Practices

### Taking Clear Attendance Photos

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Stand in front of a plain background
- Face the camera directly
- Make sure your face is well-lit
- Remove sunglasses and hats
- Keep the tablet steady
- Take photo in landscape mode

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Stand with bright light behind you
- Wear items that cover your face
- Move while taking the photo
- Take photo in dark areas

[Screenshot here]

### Writing Effective Agenda Proposals

‚úÖ **Good Example:**

**Title:** Purchase New Fire Extinguishers

**Description:** We need to buy 10 new fire extinguishers for the mine site. The old ones expired last month. Budget needed: ‚Ç±50,000. Urgent for safety compliance.

**Why it's good:**
- Clear title
- Explains the problem
- States what's needed
- Gives budget
- Explains importance

‚ùå **Bad Example:**

**Title:** Fire stuff

**Description:** Need to buy things.

**Why it's bad:**
- Vague title
- No details
- No budget
- No explanation

### When to Use Voice Recordings

‚úÖ **Use recordings for:**
- Complex discussions that need review
- Important decisions that need exact words
- Technical explanations
- Guest speaker presentations
- Training sessions

‚ùå **Don't use recordings for:**
- Every single meeting (too much storage)
- Meetings with sensitive information
- Very short meetings
- Informal chats

üí° **Best Practice:** Record key discussions and decisions, not the entire meeting.

### Keeping Organized Notes

‚úÖ **Tips:**
- Write notes during or right after the meeting
- Use clear titles like "Q2 Meeting - May 10 - Safety Discussion"
- Include date and topic
- Write main points as bullet points
- Note who said what
- Note any action items assigned to you

**Example:**
```
Title: Q2 Meeting - May 10 - Budget Review
Date: May 10, 2025

Main Points:
- Budget increased by 10%
- New project approved
- Deadline: June 30

My Action Items:
- Submit report by May 20
- Attend training on May 15
```

## Troubleshooting

### What to Do If You Can't Login

**Problem:** Error message when logging in

**Solutions to Try:**

1. **Check your spelling**
   - Type slowly and carefully
   - Username is usually case-sensitive
   - Make sure CAPS LOCK is off

2. **Check your internet**
   - Make sure you have WiFi or data
   - Try opening a web browser
   - If no internet, connect first

3. **Try again**
   - Close the app completely
   - Restart your tablet
   - Open the app again

4. **Ask for help**
   - Contact your administrator

   - They can check if your account is active

### What to Do If You Can't Upload a Photo

**Problem:** Camera won't work or photo won't save

**Solutions:**

1. **Check camera permission**
   - Go to tablet Settings
   - Find "Apps" or "Applications"
   - Find "MGB MRFC Manager"
   - Make sure Camera permission is ON

2. **Check storage space**
   - Make sure tablet has free space
   - Delete old photos or files
   - Try again

3. **Restart the camera**
   - Close the app
   - Open your tablet's camera app
   - Take a test photo
   - If it works, go back to MGB app

4. **Check internet**
   - Photo needs to upload to server
   - Make sure you have connection
   - Wait for WiFi if needed

### What to Do If a Recording Won't Play

**Problem:** Can't hear the recording or it won't start

**Solutions:**

1. **Check volume**
   - Turn up tablet volume
   - Check if muted
   - Plug in headphones to test

   - Need good connection
   - Try again with better WiFi

3. **Close other apps**
   - Too many apps might slow things down
   - Close apps you're not using
   - Try playing again

4. **Wait and retry**
   - Server might be busy
   - Wait a minute
   - Tap play again

## Glossary

**MRFC (Multi-Partite Monitoring Team)**
- A group that monitors mining companies
- Includes government, community, and company representatives
- Ensures companies follow environmental rules

**Agenda**
- A list of topics to discuss in a meeting
- Like a menu of what the meeting will cover
- Helps everyone prepare

**Proponent**
- A mining company or operator
- The business being monitored
- Required to submit reports and documents

**Compliance**
- Following all the rules and requirements
- Submitting required documents on time
- Meeting environmental and safety standards

**Quarter (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)**
- A 3-month period
- Q1 = January to March
- Q2 = April to June
- Q3 = July to September
- Q4 = October to December

**Proposal**
- A suggestion for a topic to discuss
- Submitted by users
- Needs admin approval

**Minutes**
- Written summary of a meeting
- Records what was discussed
- Records decisions made
- Official record for future reference

**Status**
- The current state of something
- Examples:
  - Draft = Not yet finalized
  - Published = Ready and shared
  - Approved = Accepted
  - Denied = Not accepted
  - Completed = Finished

**Super Admin**
- Person with full control
- Can create and manage users
- Can do everything in the app
- Usually IT staff or top management

**Admin (Administrator)**
- Person who manages the app
- Can create meetings and documents
- Can approve proposals
- Cannot create other admins

**User (Regular User)**
- Basic level of access
- Can view information
- Can make proposals
- Can log attendance
- Cannot manage other users or create meetings

**Upload**
- Sending a file from your tablet to the server
- Like mailing a document

**Download**
- Getting a file from the server to your tablet
- Like receiving a document

**Dashboard**
- The main screen you see after login
- Shows summary and quick access buttons
- Like a control panel

**Tab**
- A section at the top of the screen
- Tap to switch between different views
- Like tabs in a folder
